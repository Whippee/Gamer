Marketing Masters

Marketing Masters are an apprentice team currently working on their first software project for children. The Marketing Masters team are people such as:

  1. Ethan Whyley, the leader
  2. Tilly Hulme, the questionaire
  3. Nathan Jackson, the resource manager
  4. Leo Woolnough, the motivator
  5. Lucy Cartwright, the idea genorator
  6. Zac Colligon, the reporter

Marketing Masters are working on a childrens product named "Legacy". Legacy is a fun-filled way to help children with their spellings and boost their imagination. In Legacy, you can help the most magical creatures you could ever see reach the golden castle. If you can not get a spelling right, do not be blue. Legacy is a learning platform so each spelling you get wrong will go in your learning jar.
